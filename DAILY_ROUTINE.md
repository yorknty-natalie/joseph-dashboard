# Daily Retail Search Instruction

Every day at 09:00 AM, perform the following:

1.  **Search Google Trends** for "US Retail" top searches over the last 24 hours.
2.  **Search News** for the following 10 retailers (last 24 hours):
    - Kohl's, Target, Macy's, Dillard's, Belk, Bealls, Walmart, Ross, Burlington, TJ Maxx.
3.  **Update `Project/Dashboard/RETAIL_HISTORY.md`**: Append a new section for the date with the findings.
4.  **Update `Project/Dashboard/retail_data.js`**: Update the `retailData` object with the latest 4-5 trends and the most significant news headlines.
5.  **Commit and Push** to GitHub.
6.  **Notify Joseph** via Telegram that the daily update is complete.
